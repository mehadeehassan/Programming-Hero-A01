Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

What's included in my ticket? +

Your ticket includes access to all keynote sessions, workshops, networking events, meals during the conference, and digital recordings of all talks for 30 days after the event.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost, but refunds are not guaranteed.

Will sessions be recorded? +

Yes, all main-stage talks will be recorded and made available to registered attendees. Workshop recordings depend on speaker permission.

Is there a dress code? +

DevConf is casual. Wear whatever makes you comfortable, most attendees show up in jeans, hoodies, and conference t-shirts.

Do you offer student or group discounts? +

Yes, students get 40% off with a valid student ID, and groups of 5 or more qualify for our Team pricing plan above.
